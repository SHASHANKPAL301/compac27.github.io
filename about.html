ABOUT US

Compac Technologies India Limited (CTIL) was established in year 2002 as a channel partner of Industrial Product Range of Specialty Lubricants, Adhesives, Sealants & Coatings, Specialty Fluids & Equipment, PPE & Safety, Anchor Fasteners and Pneumatic Tools.

  • Since then, company has progressively enriched & diversified its product range, expanded its target to every industry sector and extended its portfolio to a number of new industrial solutions, beyond products, in order to effectively meet the ever evolving needs of industry
  • Industry can count on our highly technically competent sales & service team, made up of skilled professionals able to provide timely and precise solutions to customer’s need.

    • CTIL is forever committed to establishing strong relationships, based on trust and mutual benefits, with the most renowned and world’s leading product manufacturers, in order to offer an unlimited range of industrial solutions with most favorable conditions.
